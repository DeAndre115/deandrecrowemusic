About Me

About Me

Hi, My name is DeAndre Crowe and I am a Pianist.

As a 6-year old, I started playing the piano and my love for music has grow deeper ever since. I later taught myselfhow to play guitar, harmonica, accordion, and took up trumpet when I joined the band at school. Today I spend mostof my time practicing my instruments and teaching others to play music in their own way.

Creating my own music on the piano is by far my favorite thing to do. My heart’s desire is to help others find the joy indoing the same. Playing music can make you feel like you’re on top of the world; it can send shivers down your spine;it can make you feel things you've never felt before.

But when you make it yourself it opens a whole new world.

DeAndre Crowe

“Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the mind, flight to the imagination and life to everything.” ― Plato

About DeAndre

DeAndre is a professional musician specializing in improvisation. Spending over 14 years studying music , his love for music is always growing. Today he centers his life around finding new opportunities to perform and teach music.
